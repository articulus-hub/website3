Our Journey

  • 2011 Founder Saurya creates the 1st adult size humanoid robot of India at IIT Kharagpur. A system with advanced motion control systems
  • 2013 Saurya is part of one of India’s earliest Make in India for World Medical device with Philips
  • 2019 Founder joins YC funded Deep tech Robotic Microscopy start up Morphle Labs as head of Research and development
